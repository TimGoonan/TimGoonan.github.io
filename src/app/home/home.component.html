What is SlipSlo

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

SlipSlo is a free site that allows users to discuss and argue controversial topics in today's society.

The concept is easy, you choose a topic you want to discuss and the side that you will primarily be arguing. For example, you're a strong supporter of pro life and you have a research paper on abortion, but you need some viewpoints from a pro-choice standpoint, well you came to the right place. Just select abortion and choose pro life. You will then be matched up with someone with an opposite viewpoint in a private chatbox. The rest is on the two of you.

The Details

What's the difference between Serious and Savage?

And what's up with Voting and Submitting?

Serious

To join Serious you need a username, which will be explained later. There is no age limit on Serious so the discussion has to be appropriate. Nothing vulgar. There are moderators. If you aren't taking it serious you can be reported. This feature will be coming in the future.

Savage

Everything is Anonymous. That means there are no rules, no moderation, and no usernames. Anything goes besides having to be 18+.

Submitting

I'm sure you'll get bored discussing the same topics over and over again. That's why there is a section where you can submit topic ideas to be added to the list. They could be added for a day, a week, or become a permanent topic.

Voting

How will topics be selected from the submission process? You guessed it, users can vote on their favorite ones.

Usernames

The reason you need a username is because there will be a leaderboard! Once you click finished on the chatbox a popup will appear asking both users if they want the discussion to go public. If both users say yes, your discussion will be publicly viewable and people can vote for a certain duration on who won. Everything will be recorded in your history and on the leaderboards.
